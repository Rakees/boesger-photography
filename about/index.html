My name is Phillip Bösger, born in 1997, and I am a German photographer passionate about capturing life’s moments around the world.

My Photography Journey

Since 2020, I have been actively pursuing photography as a hobby, focusing on street photography, events, and weddings for friends.

Driven by my travels and the urge to discover something new, I have journeyed extensively in recent years to capture the world’s moments. Equipped with my Sony Alpha 7 III, a variety of lenses, filters, and refined technical skills, I am well-prepared for many shooting scenarios and eventualities.

What I Offer

  • Photography and photo editing services offered freely; I ask only for coverage of travel, accommodation, and meals.
  • Worldwide availability for one-day events.
  • Delivery of carefully selected and fully edited images post-event.
  • Adaptable style tailored to each client’s preferences.

Why I Do It

As a remote software developer with a stable income, I travel the world as a digital nomad to explore new cultures and meet people, pursuing my passion for photography.

I am fully aware of how expensive photographers can be and that my services are a nice bonus and motivating factor in my photography.

I focus on quality rather than quantity, which is why I plan for only one to two events per month. Taking on more assignments would risk compromising either my primary work as a software developer or the quality of the final images. Let this be another reason why I am the right choice for you and your event.
