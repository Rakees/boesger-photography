My name is Phillip Bösger, born in 1997, and I am a German photographer passionate about capturing life’s moments around the world.
I work as a remote software developer, travel the world as a digital nomad and want to reach people with my hobbies.

My Photography Journey

Since 2020, I have been actively pursuing photography as a hobby, focusing on street photography, events, and weddings for friends.

Driven by my travels and the urge to discover something new, I have journeyed extensively in recent years to capture the world’s moments. Equipped with my Sony Alpha 7 III, a variety of lenses, filters, and refined technical skills, I am well-prepared for many shooting scenarios and eventualities.

What I Offer

  • Free event, wedding, and portrait photography – I only ask for coverage of my travelcosts
  • No hourly wage, no hidden costs.
  • Worldwide availability for one-day assignments.
  • Delivery of carefully selected, fully edited high-resolution images after each event.
  • Flexible, personalized shooting style to capture your vision.

Why I Do It

Photography is my passion – I love capturing authentic moments and sharing stories through my images.

As a full-time remote software developer, I’m financially independent from photography, allowing me to offer my services free of charge to inspire more people and make professional photography accessible.

To ensure the highest quality, I take on only one to two events per month, dedicating my full attention to each assignment.

I look forward to capturing your special event and creating images you'll cherish—let's make unforgettable memories together!
